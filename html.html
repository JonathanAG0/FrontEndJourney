Hachikō

Remarkable Loyalty

Here's a time line of Hachikō's life

  • 1923- Born at a farm in Akita Prefecture
  • 1924 - Professor Hidesaburō Ueno brought him to live in Shibuya, Tokyo
  • 1925 - Professor Ueno dies from a cerebral hemorrhage, Hachikō keeps showing up at the station waiting for him
  • 1934 - A Hachikō's statue is sculped by Teru Ando at Shibuya Station
  • 1935 - Hachikō's death

Every day at around nine in the morning, Hachikō would follow Ueno who set off to Shibuya Station to go to either the Department of Agriculture at the Imperial University or the laboratory at the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry in Nishigahara.

--Kozaburo Kobayashi
